    Araxxor Basic Guide (Courtesy of #Unknown user)

    General Knowledge

    Important Things to be Aware of

    ⬥ Araxxor and Araxxi's enrage ranges from 0-300%, each kill adding 20%, the higher the enrage the more damage they deal.

    ⬥ Bring a pheromone or use an instance so Araxxor spawns weak to the weapons you're holding.

    ⬥ Araxxor uses a special attack after every 5 normal attacks.

    ⬥ Use or before each special attack.

    ⬥ Araxxor does not die until phase 3.

    ⬥ There are three paths in total, one of which will be blocked:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Top path (minions) - Northeast

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Middle path (acid) - East

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Bottom path (darkness) - Southeast

    Special Attacks

    Special Attacks
    Cleave
    ⬥ Araxxor pulls you in and cleaves dealing heavy damage. Example     • Run away. ​
    Cocoon
    ⬥ Araxxor wraps you in a cocoon dealing constant damage. Example     • Spam left-click anywhere on your screen to free yourself.     • / beforehand reduces the time it takes to get free. ​
    Web Shield
    ⬥ Araxxor covers himself in a web, and any damage dealt during this time is reflected back to you. Example     • Stop attacking Araxxor and wait for the special to end. ​
    Egg Bomb
    ⬥ Araxxor spawns a group of eggs, and fires a slow moving projectile towards you that explodes in a 4x4 radius. Example     • Stand top of the eggs and wait for it to explode. ​
    Minion Spawn
    ⬥ Araxxor spawns 5 spiders, one of which is a special one: a pulsing spider which rapidly heals Araxxor, or a mirrorback spider which reflects damage dealt to Araxxor back to you. Example     • Stop attacking Araxxor until the special spider is killed. ​
    Instakill Spider
    ⬥ Araxxor sends out a green blob marking the ground, from which a spider spawns and chases you. Example     • Making contact with the spider results in instant death.     • Run away using / and wait for it to explode on its own. ​
    Light Spots
    ⬥ Darkness covers the room and one lit up spot appears.     • Stand in the light to avoid taking damage     • Utilize + ​
    Acidic Spider
    ⬥ Looks like the Instakill spider but does not have a bar that fills over its head.     • Harmless and best left alone.     • Exclusive to phase 3 when middle path is open. ​
    Acid Absorb
    ⬥ Araxxi absorbs leftover acid from phase 3, increasing her enrage.     • Exclusive to phase 4 when middle path is open. ​
    Acid Core
    ⬥ Excluse to phase 4, at 25k HP, Araxxi drains 50% of your adrenaline and spawns a blob that bounces around the room and deals heavy damage if it touches you.     • If it hits you, move 1 tile then stand still.

    Phase 1

    ⬥ Timegated phase - Burn the web of your choice and lower Araxxor's hp to <5000.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize + / + +

    ⬥ Phase 1 special attacks:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Cleave

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Cocoon

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Web Shield

    Phase 2

    Top Path

    ⬥ Lower Araxxor to <5000 HP and progress down the path to phase 3

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize , aim to fit + both twice. / , aim to fit +

    ⬥ Additional special attacks:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Egg Bomb - Always the first spec of this phase.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Minion Spawn

    Middle Path

    ⬥ Timegated phase - Lower Araxxor's hp to <5000.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize + / + +

    ⬥ Lure Araxxor to the acid pool, and then onto the ledge after the acid level hits ~57% to dissolve it.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• In order to have space to dodge the Cleave special attack, go all the way up the ledge, then move 1 tile west (down) before Araxxor reaches you.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize / to reach the end of the ledge quicker.

    ⬥ Additional special attacks:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Egg Bomb

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Instakill Spider

    Bottom Path

    ⬥ Timegated phase - Survive and stay in the light spots. Araxxor will charge at you and prompt you to choose a direction to dodge.

    ⬥ Move to phase 3 when the wall breaks.

    ⬥ Additional Special Attacks:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Egg Bomb

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Light Spots

    Phase 3

    ⬥ This is the phase where you finally kill Araxxor.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize + / +

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Aim to fit both + twice / + +

    ⬥ Special attacks can occur from whichever 2 paths are open.

    Phase 4

    ⬥ Kill Araxxi.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Utilize + + / + +

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- If / is on cooldown, just use thresholds until you can use it.

    ⬥ Araxxi is able to use magic and ranged attacks if you stand beyond melee distance, and attacks with the style you are NOT praying against, making prayer flicking crucial.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Resort to if deemed necessary.

    ⬥ Special Attacks

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Cleave

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Cocoon

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Light Spots

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Minion Spawn

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Acid Absorb

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Acid Core

    Additional Hp-based Mechanics

    ⬥ 50k HP:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Araxxi stops using special attacks, gains increased damage, attack speed

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Soft cap: any hits above 5k are heavily reduced.

    ⬥ At 25k HP:

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Araxxi spawns the acid core

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Utilize channeled threshold / before adrenaline drain (when Araxxi has ~27k hp).
