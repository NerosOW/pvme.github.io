    Perks

    Best PvM/Slayer Perks and Their Cheap Counterparts (Courtesy of #Hoobs and #Wh)

    This channel states the combos and positions of perks on gear, for explanations of the perks check out #perk-info.

    Starter Perks

    Decent perks to start with, generally accepted as satisfactory.

    Note: with 68 + an Extreme potion you can reasonably obtain all Starter Perks except Equilibrium 4 , which is gated behind 86 + an Extreme potion

    Weapon/Switches

    ⬥ Main Hand - Precise 6

    ⬥ Off-hand - Equilibrium 4

    ⬥ Flanking switch - Flanking 4

    ⬥ Two Hand - Precise 6 + Equilibrium 4

    ⬥ Planted Feet switch - Planted Feet

    ⬥ Statius' Warhammer - Aftershock 1

    Armour/Shield

    ⬥ Body - Biting 3 + Impatient 4

    ⬥ Legs - Crackling 4 + Enhanced Devoted 4

    ⬥ Shield - Turtling 4

    Advanced PvM Perks

    Note: Upon getting Aftershock you'll now need it on all of your weapons for that style

    Magic Armour and Shields

    ⬥ Elite Tectonic Robe Top - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

    ⬥ Elite Tectonic Robe Bottom - Impatient 4 X + Invigorating 4 X

    ⬥ Dyed Elite Tectonic Robe Bottom - Impatient 4 X + Enhanced Devoted 4

    ⬥ Cryptbloom Top - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

    ⬥ Cryptbloom Bottoms - Impatient 4 X + Invigorating 4 X

    ⬥ Merciless Kiteshield - Turtling 4

    ⬥ Arcane Spirit Shield - Lucky 4 Absorbative 2

    ⬥ Kalphite Rebounder - Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

    Magic Weapons and Switches

    Note: If you do not want Ruthless 1, and will not be using Invigorating 4 on armour, you may switch staff perks with Aftershock 4 Invigorating 2 + Precise 6 Equilibrium 2

    ⬥ / Seismic Singularity or Dyed Imperium Core - Flanking 4 Equilibrium 1

    ⬥ Fractured Staff of Armadyl - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

    ⬥ Inquisitor Staff - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

    ⬥ Ancient Lantern - Planted Feet, or Caroming 4 Planted Feet

    ⬥ Enhanced Excalibur - Planted Feet

    ⬥ Abyssal Orb - Caroming 4 Equilibrium 2

    Melee Armour and Shields

    ⬥ / ‎ ‎Custom-fit TMW Platebody/Malevolent Cuirass - Biting 4 X + Relentless 5 Crackling 4

    ⬥ ‎ ‎/ Custom-fit TMW Platelegs/Malevolent Greaves - Impatient 4 X + Enhanced Devoted 4

    ⬥ ‎ ‎/ MW Platelegs/Dyed Malevolent Greaves - Impatient 4 X + Invigorating 4 Mobile

    ⬥ Malevolent Kiteshield - Turtling 4

    Melee Weapons and Switches

    ⬥ ‎ ‎Dark Shard of Leng - Precise 6 Aftershock 1

    ⬥ / ‎ ‎Off-Hand Drygore or Off-Hand Khopesh - Flanking 4 Equilibrium 1

    ⬥ Zaros Godsword - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

    ⬥ Terrasaur Maul - Aftershock 4 Equilibrium 2 + Precise 6 Ruthless 1

    ⬥ Noxious Scythe - Aftershock 4 Precise 2 + Equilibrium 4 Ruthless 3

    Ranged Armour and Shields
