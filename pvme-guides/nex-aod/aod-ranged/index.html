    Aod ranged

    AoD Ranged (Courtesy of #Hells)

    This section will detail the rotations, role responsibilities and strategies used for all ranged roles at AoD.

    Chinner

    Role Responsibilities

    The chinner is responsible for clearing minions in p3 with mechanised chinchompas as well as applying the statius warhammer debuff in p1 at the very beginning of the fight. They are also repsonsible for backup basing should the base die after minions have been killed. Should base die after minions are killed, it is the chinner's job to take over.

    Preset

    It is also beneficial for chinners to use the Heightened Senses Relic as the 110% adren cap is very useful since the role overcaps on adren a lot. For the 2/4 rotation, it is required.

    Rotations

    There are 2 rotations available to a chinner. The 1/3 rotation involves the chinner using their p1 and p3, while the 2/4 rotation uses their on p2 and, depending on team speed, p4 as well. 1/3 rotation should be used for slower teams, while 2/4 should be used for faster teams to ensure a timely chincend.

    Pillars and Zaros phases have no set rot for rangers, due to adren variation between kills caused by buff and hydrix bolts as well as pillar kill speed varying hugely from team to team or even kill to kill.     ⬥ Pillars + Zaros

    Pillars and Zaros phase have no set rot for rangers, due to adren variation between kills caused by buff and hydrix bolts as well as pillar kill speed varying hugely from team to team or even kill to kill. You should aim to be in for 1st and 2nd pillars, re at third pillar for uptime on 3rd to 4th pillar, and finally re- on during Zaros phase. When travelling between pillars, minimising tick loss is different to other roles due to wanting to keep your ecb spec, so you can't use bd to do this. To work around this, you're going to need to double surge. To do this, start spam clicking towards the next pillar before doing your ability, then the same tick you do the ability, surge and then surge again. By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it.

    Range Base (Courtesy of #Unknown user)

    Role Responsibilities

    The responsibilities of a range base are as follows:

    ⬥ Tank boss for the duration of the fight.

    ⬥ Apply the vulnerability debuff on phase 1, phase 3, last pillar and zaros.

    ⬥ Lure at an appropriate time at the end of p3 so as to avoid giving the team shadow traps at pillars.

    ⬥ Apply Statius' Warhammer debuff during pillars.
