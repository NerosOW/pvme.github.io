    Nex solo ranged ecb

    Solo Nex (Courtesy of #Hoobs & #Frog)

    Introduction

    When Nex spawns for the first time in a custom instance it will be praying Deflect Magic for the majority of the kill, this is why Range only kills are the meta for fast consistent kills. After every kill you will teleport out of the instance and re-enter in order to force Nex to always be praying Deflect Mage

    Note: It is strongly advised to complete the Ritual of the Mahjarrat quest in order to get access to the Jas boost which gives a 5% damage boost in GWD1 (including Nex). This can be done via Fairy ring code D.K.Q.

    Positioning

    Note: The white square represents the player, the red square represents Nex and the pink square where to click. The arrow points in the general direction of movement for the phase

    Smoke Phase

    ⬥ Stand on the west side, once she’s phased click on Fumus and after your targetcycle on Nex and click mid while using .

    Shadow Phase

    ⬥ During shadow make your way to the east safe spot while phasing Nex

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Once phased, try to stay MD / 2 squares away from Nex so she can reach you instantly and begin siphoning.

    Blood Phase

    ⬥ During her siphon, make your way over to the eastern safe spot whilst staying at an even number of squares so that if she does run to you, she won’t run underneath you which could delay her attacks which could mean she won’t sacrifice if everything lines up poorly.

    ⬥ At the eastern safespot you can click Cruor during your Rapid Fire and it won’t switch unless your ability ends or Nex phases.

    Ice Phase

    ⬥ Once Cruor is dead, Surge to Glacies and stand MD to him to stall Nex autos.

    Zaros Phase

    ⬥ Try to get in MD of Nex fast to prevent book walks.

    Phase Notes

    Smoke Phase Notes

    ⬥ Throw your bomb right before the kill is about to start.

    ⬥ Because of the travel time of rapid fire you are able to go into the fumus safespot at around 166k lp to save the tick running to fumus.

    Shadow Phase Notes

    ⬥ If you snipe at around 126.5k lp you won't need another ability to finish her off. If you snipe needlessly (can happen after getting a ruby auto) you can forgo landing that snipe and instead do a 6t auto on the minion along with greater ricochet for a fair chance of killing umbra,

    Blood Phase Notes

    ⬥ Be sure to let her Sacrifice so you get a contain first on Ice phase

    ⬥ In rare cases it is possible to have to your Adrenaline Potion in order to use Rapid Fire . In this case you will need to improvise your Zaros rotation.

    ⬥ Practice your Snipe timing so that it hits right when Nex is attackable, rather than healing her. You can snipe when her Wings go up a 3rd time.

    Ice Phase Notes

    ⬥ If you get prisoned before targeting Glacies you can target cycle to attack him.

    Zaros Phase Notes

    ⬥ Disruption shield may be used if you are low on food.

    ⬥ You can run Nex if low on food, but don't need to (and will end up walking book procs). It's optimal to do so if you notice her SS is active.
