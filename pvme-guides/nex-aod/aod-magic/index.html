    This section will detail the rotations, role responsibilities and strategies used for all mage roles at AoD.

    Magic Minion Tank

    Role Responsibilities

    Magic minion tanks are usually umbra. They are responsible for applying in phase 1 as well as vulning umbra and pillars 1, 2 and 3 (if on umbra). They should lure their minion to mid after p2 and relure if necessary.

    Preset

    ⬥ If you do not own a Fractured Staff of Armadyl , replace with an Inquisitor's Staff .

    ⬥ - Used on pillars. If comfortable with less food, a sirenic switch can be brought, otherwise unequip tectonic for the spec. May also be replaced by an Eldritch Crossbow with SGB Eof and Hydrix bakriminel bolts (e) but this is not highly recommended due to inventory space constraints.

    ⬥ Armadyl Battlestaff EoF should only be brought if you have a Fractured Staff of Armadyl .

    It is beneficial for mages to use the persistent rage relic to ensure that they do not lose adren during their pre-detonate.

    Magic Base

    At the time of writing (12/10/21), solo mage base is considered meta at AoD.

    Role Responsibilities

    The responsibilities of a mage base are as follows:

    ⬥ Tank boss for the duration of the fight.

    ⬥ Apply the vulnerability debuff on phase 1, phase 3, last pillar and zaros.

    ⬥ Lure at an appropriate time at the end of p3 so as to avoid giving the team shadow traps at pillars.

    ⬥ Apply Statius' Warhammer debuff during pillars.

    Preset

    ⬥ If you do not own a Fractured Staff of Armadyl , replace with an Inquisitor's Staff .

    ⬥ - Used on pillars. If comfortable with less food, a sirenic armour switch can be brought, otherwise unequip tectonic for the spec. May also be replaced by an Eldritch Crossbow with SGB Eof and Hydrix bakriminel bolts (e) but this is not highly recommended due to inventory space constraints.

    ⬥ Armadyl Battlestaff EoF should only be brought if you have a Fractured Staff of Armadyl .

    Beginner Rotation

    This part of the guide is aimed at bases who are still new to the boss and the magic combat style in general. It does not make use of 4ticking or spell swapping and does not require a Fractured Staff of Armadyl. The rotation is best suited for teams whose kills take longer than 2:30 - 2:40 minutes. As with all beginner guides, this should only be used as your stepping stone into basing at AoD. You should eventually try to progress to the other rotations listed in the channel since they are much better suited for meta teams.
