    4-Man AoD (Courtesy of #Unknown user)

    Introduction

    This is a guide for 4-man Nex - Angel of Death. It is assumed that you know how to deal with mechanics in 7-man AoD, described in #aod.

    Due to the meta heavily favouring magic at the time of writing, this guide will assume that the team consists of 2 magic MTs, a magic base and either a free mage or a chinner. The chinner and free mage will be referred to as free dps throughout the guide. The advantage of having a chinner is that killing the minions is more consistent, which allows more room for error. The advantage of a mage is that, if everything is done right, it's faster.

    Mechanics

    Phase 1 (3,000,000 - 2,100,000 HP)

    ⬥ Elements

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Quadrant with elements

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Tank it as you would in larger teams

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• Quadrant with reavers

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Throw sticky bombs at the reavers until AoD hits her phase hp.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Sticky bombs last 6s / 10t.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎- Before getting bound by consecutive sticky bombs, the reaver will always walk at least 1 tile.

    Note: fast teams might not require sticky bombs.

    Phase 2 (2,100,000 - 1,800,000 HP)

    Handle mechanics exactly the same as in Phase 1.

    Phase 3 (1,800,000 - 600,000 HP)

    ⬥ Smoke Pool

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎All pools taken will do 14k damage spread evenly among the people inside.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎The free dps is tasked with taking the first pool making use of a vitality potion.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎The other pools have to be tanked by at least 2 people both with >7k hp.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎Multiple people can step into the pool to reduce the damage taken per player.

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎If minions are still alive it is preferred to not have MTs take the pools as it will relocate minions.

    ⬥ Ice

    ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎• ‎ ‎If the free dps gets iced they need to be freed as their powerburst will be on cooldown.

    ⬥ All other mechanics work the same as in 7s.

    Minion Lure (Image courtesy of #Unknown user)

    To lure the minions using 2 minions tanks (UG and CF) players need to be positioned correctly. The two minion tanks need to be on the green line on the east and west side of AoD respectively. All other players need to be on the north/south line inside the green lines. This will make it so that the minions aggro on the correct targets. The minion tanks then go to the spots from where you would lure in 7s as Glacies MT and Cruor MT before surging across.

    There is no set rotation for pillars, but there are some general guidelines. Try to be under the effect of as much as possible and use whenever it's off cooldown. Due to pillars taking longer than in 7s the use of is possible.

    When travelling between pillars, minimising tick loss is different to other styles due to wanting to keep your , so you can't use to do this. To work around this, you're going to need to double surge. To do this, start spam clicking towards the next pillar before doing your ability, then the same tick you do the ability, surge and then surge again. By doing this will get you within range of the next pillar. If you aren't able to attack from that new position, make sure to stall an ability on the pillar for once you do run to it.
